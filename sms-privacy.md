THIS NINJA LLC ("THIS NINJA", "we", "us", or "our") may use SMS text messaging to communicate with individuals who have provided consent to receive text messages from us.

## What information we collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from THIS NINJA, we may collect and maintain:

- Your mobile phone number
- Your name, if you provide it
- The content of messages sent to and from you
- The date, time, and status of messages
- Your communication preferences, including opt-in and opt-out status

## How we use SMS information

We use SMS information to provide conversational business communications, including:

- Responding to inquiries
- Scheduling or confirming calls, meetings, or appointments
- Providing service-related follow-up
- Sharing direct business support communications
- Communicating about active business discussions or engagements

SMS messages from THIS NINJA are intended for low-volume, one-to-one business communication. We do not use this SMS program for bulk marketing campaigns, mass texting, or unsolicited promotional blasts.

## How we share information

We may share limited information with service providers that help us deliver messaging and operate our communications systems, but only as needed to support our business communications.

**Mobile opt-in information will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.**

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our business, systems, users, or others.

## Data retention

We may retain SMS-related information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate our communications, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

## Your choices

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. After you opt out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from that number unless you opt in again.

You can reply **HELP** if you need assistance.
