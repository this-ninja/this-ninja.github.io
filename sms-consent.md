THIS NINJA LLC requests SMS consent before sending text messages for conversational business communications.

## How consent works

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from THIS NINJA by providing your mobile number and giving clear permission to receive text messages from us in a business context, such as through a web form, during a call, or during another direct interaction.

By opting in, you agree to receive conversational SMS messages from THIS NINJA LLC at the mobile number you provide. These messages may include replies to inquiries, scheduling or confirmation messages, service follow-up, and direct business support communications.

**Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out.**

## Important consent notice

Your consent applies only to SMS messages from THIS NINJA related to your direct interaction with us. We do not use this SMS program for bulk marketing campaigns, mass texting, or automated promotional alerts.
